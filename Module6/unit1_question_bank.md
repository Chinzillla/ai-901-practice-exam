# Module 6: Introduction to AI-Powered Information Extraction Concepts

## Unit 1 Question Bank: Introduction

---

### 1. What is AI-powered information extraction?

A. Extracting structured fields from unstructured content  
B. Reading text aloud  
C. Detecting objects in traffic videos only  
D. Sorting email by sender manually  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Information extraction identifies relevant fields and values.

---

### 2. Which document is commonly used in financial extraction scenarios?

A. Invoice  
B. Weather forecast only  
C. Keyboard manual only  
D. Audio ringtone  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Invoices contain structured business fields such as vendor, dates, and totals.

---

### 3. Which field might be extracted from a receipt?

A. Merchant name  
B. Camera resolution  
C. Model temperature  
D. Prompt token limit only  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Receipts often include merchant, purchase, and payment details.

---

### 4. Which industry may extract patient details from records?

A. Healthcare  
B. Retail checkout only  
C. Computer gaming only  
D. Weather forecasting only  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Medical records can contain patient and clinical information.

---

### 5. True or False: Information extraction can support workflow automation.

**Answer:** True  
**Rationale:** Extracted data can be passed into business systems.

---

### 6. True or False: Information extraction applies only to perfectly structured databases.

**Answer:** False  
**Rationale:** It is designed for unstructured or semi-structured sources.
