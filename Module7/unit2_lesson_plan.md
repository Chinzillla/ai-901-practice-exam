# Module 7: Plan and Prepare to Develop AI Solutions on Azure

## Unit 2: What Is AI?

### Source

Microsoft Learn: [What is AI?](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/prepare-azure-ai-development/2-what-is-ai)

### Unit Overview

This unit reviews AI capabilities that developers can integrate into applications, including generative AI and agents, natural language processing, computer speech, computer vision, and information extraction.

### Learning Objectives

- Describe AI as software capabilities that exhibit human-like behavior.
- Identify common AI capabilities for developers.
- Match AI capabilities to application scenarios.
- Explain why choosing capabilities helps determine required services.

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| AI capability | A specific AI function such as language, speech, vision, or extraction. |
| Generative AI | AI that creates original responses or content from prompts. |
| Agentic AI | AI solutions where agents use instructions and tools to perform tasks. |
| NLP | AI techniques for understanding and analyzing language. |
| Computer speech | AI capabilities for recognizing and synthesizing speech. |
| Computer vision | AI capabilities for interpreting visual input. |
| Information extraction | AI capabilities for extracting key data from content. |

### Lesson Flow

#### 1. AI in Modern Applications

Explain that modern AI solutions are often based on machine learning models that learn semantic relationships from large data sets.

#### 2. Capability Review

Review generative AI, agents, NLP, speech, vision, and information extraction.

#### 3. Capability Selection

Explain that identifying needed capabilities helps developers choose services to provision and configure.

### Suggested Activities

- Learners match business requirements to AI capabilities.
- Learners design a small app and list required AI capabilities.

### Checks for Understanding

1. What is an AI capability?
2. Which capability supports voice input?
3. Which capability supports image interpretation?
4. Why does capability selection matter?
