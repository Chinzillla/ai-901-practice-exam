# Module 7: Plan and Prepare to Develop AI Solutions on Azure

## Unit 9: Summary

### Source

Microsoft Learn: [Summary](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/prepare-azure-ai-development/9-summary)

### Unit Overview

This unit summarizes planning and preparation concepts for AI application development on Azure, with Microsoft Foundry as the preferred platform for many AI solutions.

### Learning Objectives

- Summarize key AI development planning considerations.
- Review Microsoft Foundry and Foundry Tools.
- Identify developer tools and SDKs.
- Connect responsible AI principles to development planning.

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Planning | Evaluating requirements, tools, services, and risks before implementation. |
| Microsoft Foundry | Azure AI development platform. |
| Responsible AI | Principles and practices for trustworthy AI systems. |

### Lesson Flow

#### 1. Review Planning

Summarize why AI development requires planning across services, models, tools, prompts, and code.

#### 2. Review Foundry

Summarize the role of projects, tools, portal, and SDK.

#### 3. Review Responsible AI

Summarize why responsible AI should shape the project from the beginning.

### Suggested Activities

- Learners write a short AI project readiness checklist.
- Learners choose tools for a sample development project.

### Checks for Understanding

1. Why is planning important for AI projects?
2. What is Microsoft Foundry?
3. What are Foundry Tools?
4. Why should responsible AI be considered early?
