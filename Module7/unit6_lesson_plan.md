# Module 7: Plan and Prepare to Develop AI Solutions on Azure

## Unit 6: Responsible AI

### Source

Microsoft Learn: [Responsible AI](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/prepare-azure-ai-development/6-responsible-ai)

### Unit Overview

This unit reviews responsible AI principles for developers building AI-enabled applications. Learners focus on fairness, reliability and safety, privacy and security, inclusiveness, transparency, and accountability.

### Learning Objectives

- Explain why responsible AI is important for developers.
- Identify Microsoft's responsible AI principles.
- Match responsible AI principles to development scenarios.
- Recognize why probabilistic models require careful testing and governance.

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Fairness | Treating people fairly and avoiding unjust bias. |
| Reliability and safety | Ensuring systems behave dependably and avoid harm. |
| Privacy and security | Protecting data and customer content. |
| Inclusiveness | Designing AI to benefit diverse users. |
| Transparency | Making AI purpose, limitations, and behavior understandable. |
| Accountability | Ensuring people remain responsible for AI systems. |

### Lesson Flow

#### 1. Why Responsible AI Matters

Explain that human-like AI can lead users to trust outputs, so developers must reduce risk and harm.

#### 2. Principles Review

Teach fairness, reliability and safety, privacy and security, inclusiveness, transparency, and accountability.

#### 3. Development Lifecycle

Discuss training data, testing, confidence thresholds, safeguards, disclosure, and governance.

### Suggested Activities

- Learners match responsible AI principles to examples.
- Learners identify safeguards for a loan approval or facial recognition system.

### Checks for Understanding

1. What principle addresses bias?
2. What principle addresses personal data?
3. Why should users understand limitations?
4. Who remains accountable for AI systems?
