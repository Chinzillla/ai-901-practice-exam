# Module 1: Introduction to AI Concepts

## Unit 7 Question Bank: Responsible AI

These questions are authored from the unit's Microsoft Learn concepts and rewritten as exam-style practice items. Answer choices are intentionally paraphrased instead of copied from the lesson text.

---

### 1. A loan-screening AI performs worse for one demographic group than another. Which responsible AI principle is most relevant?

A. reliability and safety  
B. privacy and security  
C. accountability  
D. Fairness  

**Answer:** D  
**Rationale:** This scenario matches Responsible AI as described in the unit.

---

### 2. What is the main purpose of "fairness"?

A. protecting data and controlling access throughout the AI solution  
B. making people responsible for AI decisions, oversight, and governance  
C. designing AI systems to avoid unfair treatment of people or groups  
D. ensuring AI behaves dependably and does not create unreasonable harm  

**Answer:** C  
**Rationale:** "fairness" is a core idea in this unit, and the correct option paraphrases its role.

---

### 3. A healthcare assistant must avoid unsafe recommendations and be tested before use. Which principle is central?

A. Speech synthesis  
B. Reliability and safety for this scenario  
C. Prompt engineering  
D. Object detection  

**Answer:** B  
**Rationale:** The correct option is the unit concept that best fits the stated requirement.

### 4. How do transparency and accountability work together?

A. Transparency explains how the system works; accountability assigns responsibility for its use and outcomes.  
B. Transparency hides model behavior, while accountability removes human review.  
C. Both mean the model is always correct.  
D. Accountability only applies to image generation.  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Responsible AI includes both understandable systems and human governance.

### 5. What should a learner remember about "reliability and safety"?

A. a process for changing the visual theme of the development portal  
B. a manual-only activity that does not use an AI model  
C. a security shortcut that removes the need for access controls  
D. ensuring AI behaves dependably and does not create unreasonable harm as described in this unit  

**Answer:** D  
**Rationale:** "reliability and safety" is tested by recognizing its purpose, not by memorizing wording from the page.

### 6. Why should privacy and security be considered early in an AI solution?

**Rationale:** The unit includes privacy and security as core responsible AI considerations.

### 7. Which two practices support responsible AI? Choose two.
