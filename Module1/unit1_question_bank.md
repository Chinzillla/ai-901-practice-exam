# Module 1: Introduction to AI Concepts

## Unit 1 Question Bank: Introduction to AI

These questions reinforce beginner knowledge about why AI concepts matter, how AI workloads are categorized, and how conceptual understanding supports AI-901 exam readiness.

---

### 1. What is the main purpose of an introductory AI concepts unit?

A. To provide a high-level foundation for understanding what AI can do  
B. To teach advanced machine learning mathematics  
C. To replace all programming courses  
D. To certify a learner as an expert AI engineer  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Introductory AI units help learners understand core capabilities and vocabulary before moving into detailed implementation.

---

### 2. Which statement best describes artificial intelligence at a beginner level?

A. A broad set of capabilities that help systems perform tasks associated with human-like reasoning, perception, or language  
B. A single database product used only for storing text  
C. A hardware component found only in servers  
D. A replacement for every software application  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** AI covers many capabilities, including language, speech, vision, generation, and decision support.

---

### 3. Which of the following is an example of an AI workload?

A. Computer vision  
B. Disk defragmentation  
C. Cable management  
D. File compression only  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Computer vision is an AI workload focused on analyzing visual information.

---

### 4. Why is conceptual understanding important before studying AI implementation?

A. It helps learners identify the right AI capability for a given problem  
B. It eliminates the need to understand any services or tools  
C. It guarantees that every model will be accurate  
D. It prevents learners from using cloud services  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** AI-901 often expects learners to match scenarios with the appropriate AI workload or capability.

---

### 5. Which topic is more conceptual than implementation-focused?

A. Understanding that speech recognition converts spoken language into text  
B. Writing authentication code for a cloud resource  
C. Installing a Python package  
D. Configuring a deployment pipeline  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Recognizing what speech recognition does is conceptual; coding and configuration are implementation details.

---

### 6. Which topic is more implementation-focused than conceptual?

A. Choosing an SDK to call an AI service  
B. Recognizing that generative AI can create text  
C. Identifying computer vision as an AI workload  
D. Understanding that responsible AI reduces risk  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** SDK selection is part of building an application, while the other options are conceptual AI ideas.

---

### 7. A learner wants to prepare for AI-901. What should they be able to do with AI scenarios?

A. Identify which AI capability best fits the scenario  
B. Memorize every line of source documentation  
C. Avoid learning AI vocabulary  
D. Skip all conceptual topics  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Exam questions commonly describe requirements and ask learners to recognize the correct AI concept or workload.

---

### 8. Which capability is most closely associated with analyzing images?

A. Computer vision  
B. Sentiment analysis  
C. Speech synthesis  
D. Text translation  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Computer vision focuses on visual content such as images and video.

---

### 9. Which capability is most closely associated with analyzing written language?

A. Natural language processing  
B. Computer cooling  
C. Network switching  
D. File archiving  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Natural language processing helps AI systems work with human language in text or speech-related scenarios.

---

### 10. Which capability is most closely associated with creating new text, images, code, or other content?

A. Generative AI  
B. Optical storage  
C. Manual data entry  
D. Disk formatting  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Generative AI creates new content based on prompts and learned patterns.

---

### 11. Which capability is most closely associated with spoken input or spoken output?

A. Speech AI  
B. Computer vision  
C. Data backup  
D. Spreadsheet sorting  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Speech AI includes capabilities such as speech recognition and speech synthesis.

---

### 12. Which capability is most closely associated with extracting values from documents or images?

A. Information extraction  
B. Screen brightness control  
C. File compression  
D. Network addressing  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Information extraction identifies useful data values from unstructured sources such as forms, documents, or images.

---

### 13. Why should responsible AI be introduced early in an AI course?

A. AI solutions can create risks that should be considered during design and use  
B. Responsible AI applies only after an exam is passed  
C. Responsible AI is unrelated to real AI systems  
D. Responsible AI is only about making models faster  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Responsible AI helps learners think about fairness, reliability, privacy, safety, transparency, and accountability.

---

### 14. A company wants to summarize long reports for employees. Which broad AI area is most relevant?

A. Language or generative AI  
B. Cable installation  
C. Operating system patching  
D. Printer maintenance  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Summarization involves language understanding and may use generative AI.

---

### 15. A learner watches a video for a quick overview but needs more detail for review. Which format is often better for careful exam study?

A. Text-based learning material  
B. Random guessing  
C. Ignoring the module  
D. Hardware documentation only  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Text-based material is easier to reread, reference, and use for focused study.

---

### 16. True or False: Introductory AI learning should always begin with advanced model mathematics.

**Answer:** False  
**Rationale:** Beginners should first understand AI capabilities, scenarios, and terminology before advanced math or implementation details.

---

### 17. True or False: AI is a broad field that includes multiple workload types.

**Answer:** True  
**Rationale:** AI includes areas such as generative AI, natural language, speech, computer vision, and information extraction.

---

### 18. True or False: AI-901 preparation benefits from learning how to match business scenarios to AI capabilities.

**Answer:** True  
**Rationale:** Scenario recognition is important because exam questions often describe a need and ask for the correct AI capability.

---

### 19. True or False: Text-based learning content can support review because learners can reread specific explanations.

**Answer:** True  
**Rationale:** Text is useful for review, note-taking, and careful study.

---

### 20. True or False: Conceptual AI knowledge is unnecessary if a learner knows how to write code.

**Answer:** False  
**Rationale:** Coding skill is useful, but AI-901 also expects learners to understand concepts, workloads, and when to use specific capabilities.
