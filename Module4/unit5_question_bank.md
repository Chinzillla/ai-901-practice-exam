# Module 4: Introduction to AI Speech Concepts

## Unit 5 Question Bank: Exercise - Explore AI Speech

These questions reinforce the exercise concepts of speech-to-text and text-to-speech in a chatbot.

---

### 1. In a voice chatbot, what does speech-to-text do?

A. Converts the user's spoken input into text  
B. Converts images into labels  
C. Extracts fields from forms  
D. Generates object boxes  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Speech-to-text transcribes spoken input.

---

### 2. In a voice chatbot, what does text-to-speech do?

A. Converts the bot's written response into audio  
B. Converts audio into written input  
C. Detects sentiment only  
D. Removes all captions  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Text-to-speech lets the chatbot respond aloud.

---

### 3. Which factor can affect speech recognition during the exercise?

A. Background noise  
B. Image resolution only  
C. Document font size only  
D. Spreadsheet formulas  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Noise can make speech harder to transcribe accurately.

---

### 4. Why should learners observe both transcription and spoken output?

A. To understand both sides of a voice conversation  
B. To ignore the chatbot's behavior  
C. To replace all text interfaces  
D. To test image classification  

**Answer:** A  
**Rationale:** Voice experiences often combine recognition and synthesis.

---

### 5. True or False: A chatbot can use both speech-to-text and text-to-speech.

**Answer:** True  
**Rationale:** It can listen to the user and respond aloud.

---

### 6. True or False: Text alternatives are unnecessary when speech is available.

**Answer:** False  
**Rationale:** Some users may prefer or require text-based interaction.
