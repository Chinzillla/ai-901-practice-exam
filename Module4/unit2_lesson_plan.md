# Module 4: Introduction to AI Speech Concepts

## Unit 2: Speech-Enabled Solutions

### Source

Microsoft Learn: [Speech-enabled solutions](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/introduction-ai-speech/2-speech-solutions)

### Unit Overview

This unit explores why developers add speech to AI applications and agents. Learners review business and user benefits, common speech recognition scenarios, common speech synthesis scenarios, combined voice experiences, and implementation considerations.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this unit, learners should be able to:

- Identify benefits of speech-enabled AI solutions.
- Recognize common speech recognition scenarios.
- Recognize common speech synthesis scenarios.
- Describe scenarios that combine both capabilities.
- Identify key considerations before implementing speech.

### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Speech recognition | Converting audio into text. |
| Speech synthesis | Converting text into audio. |
| IVR | Interactive voice response systems that guide callers through spoken interaction. |
| Latency | The delay between input and response. |
| Accessibility standard | A guideline that helps make applications usable for people with disabilities. |

### Lesson Flow

#### 1. Benefits of Speech

Review accessibility, productivity, user experience, and global audience reach.

#### 2. Speech Recognition Scenarios

Discuss customer service transcription, voice assistants, meeting transcription, and healthcare documentation.

#### 3. Speech Synthesis Scenarios

Discuss conversational AI responses, accessibility, alerts, e-learning, and media.

#### 4. Combined Scenarios

Review voice-driven support, IVR systems, language learning, and voice-controlled vehicles.

#### 5. Implementation Considerations

Discuss audio quality, language and dialect support, privacy and compliance, latency, and alternative text-based methods.

### Suggested Activities

#### Activity 1: Scenario Sort

Learners sort scenarios into recognition, synthesis, or both.

#### Activity 2: Risk Review

Learners choose a speech scenario and identify privacy, latency, or accessibility concerns.

### Checks for Understanding

1. What are benefits of speech-enabled AI?
2. Which scenarios use speech recognition?
3. Which scenarios use speech synthesis?
4. Why should apps provide alternative input and output methods?
